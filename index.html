CalPro
A calendar-first calorie & macro tracker
SwiftUI Gemini + RAG-style parsing On-device data

CalPro lets people log meals in seconds — type "2 butter naan with tandoori chicken" and it parses the dish, matches it against bundled Indian nutrition datasets (IFCT / INDB), and breaks down calories and macros instantly. An optional Gemini parser handles messy free-text, while progress rings, streaks and monthly insights keep users motivated.
